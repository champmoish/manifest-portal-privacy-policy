Privacy Policy for Manifest Portal

Last Updated: [11/17/2025]

Introduction

Manifest Portal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Manifest Portal is designed with privacy in mind. All data you create within the App is stored locally on your device using local storage technology. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

The following types of data are stored locally on your device:

  • Dream Journal Entries: Your dreams, goals, action steps, target dates, emotions, and gratitude notes
  • Vision Board Images: Photos you upload for your vision board
  • Journal Entries: Personal journal entries and reflections
  • Affirmations: Custom affirmations you create and favorite affirmations
  • Synchronicities: Records of signs and coincidences you log
  • User Statistics: XP points, level, streaks, badges, and activity history
  • Daily Teachings: Your interaction with daily teachings
  • App Preferences: Settings and preferences you configure

Permissions We Request

To provide full functionality, the App requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications Permission: To send you daily affirmations, teachings, and journal reminders at scheduled times
  • Photo/Media Access: To allow you to select and upload images for your vision board
  • Storage Access: To save images and app data locally on your device

These permissions are used solely for the App's functionality and are not used to collect or transmit data externally.

How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • We do not collect your personal information
  • We do not transmit your data to external servers
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or analytics
  • We do not track your usage or behavior

Your data remains entirely on your device and is under your control.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party packages for functionality:

  • Google Fonts: Used to display fonts within the App. Fonts may be downloaded from Google's servers, but no personal data is transmitted.
  • Flutter Local Notifications: Used to schedule and display local notifications on your device.
  • Image Picker: Used to access your device's photo library to select images for your vision board.

These services operate according to their own privacy policies, but we do not transmit any of your personal data to them.

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We recommend using device-level security measures (passcodes, biometrics) to protect your device
  • If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be deleted from your device

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the App
  • Deletion: You can delete individual items (dreams, journal entries, etc.) at any time through the App
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the App will remove all stored data from your device
  • Export: Currently, data export functionality is not available, but all data remains on your device

Children's Privacy

Manifest Portal is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you:

  • Delete individual items through the App
  • Uninstall the App (which removes all data)

We do not retain any copies of your data on external servers.

International Users

If you are using the App from outside the United States, please note that your data is stored locally on your device and is subject to the laws of your jurisdiction.
